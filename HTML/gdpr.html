Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the email mailing list of APL Germany e. V., operated through the automated list service Alisia. It informs you about what personal data is collected, for what purpose, how long it is stored, and what rights you have.

The legal basis is the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG).

1. Controller

The controller within the meaning of the GDPR is the operator of the list (APL Germany e. V.). For questions regarding data protection, please contact the list address by email: alisia@apl-germany.de.

2. What data is stored?

2.2 Message archive (Data/)

Every email sent to the list is stored in the archive (as an .eml file along with a metadata file). The archive contains the sender address, subject, date and content. It serves to provide the GETMSGS/LISTMSGS functionality for subscribers.

2.3 Log files (alisia-YYYYMMDD.jsonl)

The list service writes daily rotating log files in JSON Lines format. An entry contains a timestamp, log level, category and a message. For subscriber-related events (category SUB), the email address is additionally logged.

2.4 Action log / GDPR audit log (audit.jsonl)

All subscriber-related actions are recorded in a separate file audit.jsonl. This serves to fulfil the accountability obligation under Art. 5(2) GDPR and to safeguard your right of access (Art. 15 GDPR). Recorded events:

EventDescription
subscribe-pendingRegistration requested (confirmation email sent)
confirmDouble opt-in confirmation received
nomailDelivery paused (NOMAIL command)
mailDelivery re-enabled (MAIL command)
setmydataProfile data changed (fields and old/new values)
deletemeAccount and data deleted

You can view this data at any time using GETMYDATA. When an account is deleted (DELETEME), all your entries are permanently removed from the audit log.

5. Your rights – and how to exercise them

You can exercise your rights at any time by emailing the list address. Send an email with the relevant command in the subject line or message body.

Art. 15 GDPR – Right of access

You will receive complete information about all data stored for your email address, including the action log.

Command: GETMYDATA

Alisia will reply with an email containing your stored profile data and all logged events from the audit log.

Art. 16 GDPR – Right to rectification

You can change your name or an alternative email address.

Command: SETMYDATA with a JSON object in the message body, e. g.:

{ "name": "New Name", "alternative_email": "other@example.com" }
Possible fields: name, alternative_email.
Alisia will confirm the change by email and indicate which fields were modified.

Art. 17 GDPR – Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

Your subscription and all associated personal data will be completely deleted:

  • Your entry in the subscriber list will be removed.
  • Your address will be removed from the internal block list (if present).
  • Your sender address in already archived messages will be replaced by [deleted].
  • All your entries in the audit log (audit.jsonl) will be permanently deleted.

Command: DELETEME

The deletion itself is only noted in the current day's operational log file, which does not contain complete personal data and is not part of the GETMYDATA disclosure.

Art. 18 GDPR – Right to restriction of processing

You can pause the delivery of list emails at any time without unsubscribing.

Pause delivery: NOMAIL
Resume delivery: MAIL

Art. 21 GDPR – Right to object

You can object to the processing of your data at any time. In this case we will delete your account completely (equivalent to the DELETEME command). Please contact us by email at alisia@apl-germany.de.

Art. 77 GDPR – Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority, in particular in the member state of your habitual residence, place of work or place of the alleged infringement.

7. Disclosure to third parties

Personal data is not disclosed to third parties. Messages you send to the list are delivered to all subscribers – this corresponds to the purpose of the mailing list, which you agreed to upon registration.

8. Technical security

The list service communicates exclusively over encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) to the email server. The data files are stored on the server's operating system; access is restricted to the operator.
